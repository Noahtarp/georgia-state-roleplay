Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us

What is Georgia State Roleplay?

Georgia State Roleplay is a serious roleplay community on Roblox. We offer a semi-whitelisted server where players can engage in high-quality, immersive roleplay experiences. Our community welcomes both beginners and experienced roleplayers.

What does "semi-whitelisted" mean?

Semi-whitelisted means that while anyone can join our server, certain positions and roles require an application and approval process. For example, positions like Georgia State Patrol (GSP) and FBI are whitelisted and require an application. Regular law enforcement positions are not whitelisted.

How do I apply for a whitelisted position?

You can apply for whitelisted positions (Staff, GSP, or FBI) through our website. Simply navigate to the application section, fill out the required information, and submit your application. Our staff team will review it and respond via Discord.

What is serious roleplay?

Serious roleplay means we maintain high standards for roleplay quality. We expect players to stay in character, follow realistic scenarios, and treat roleplay interactions with respect and effort. It's not about being strictâ€”it's about creating meaningful, engaging experiences for everyone.

I'm new to roleplay. Can I still join?

Absolutely! We welcome roleplayers of all skill levels. Our community is beginner-friendly, and we have experienced members and staff who are happy to help you learn. Don't be afraid to ask questions or seek guidance.

How do I join the Discord server?

You can join our Discord server by clicking the "Join Discord" button on our website or using the invite link provided. Discord is where we coordinate events, share updates, and communicate as a community.

What positions are whitelisted?

Currently, the following positions require whitelisting: Staff, Georgia State Patrol (GSP), and FBI. Regular law enforcement positions are not whitelisted and can be accessed without an application.

How long does it take to get a response on my application?

Application review times vary depending on the number of applications and staff availability. Typically, you can expect a response within a few days to a week. You'll receive a notification via Discord when your application has been reviewed.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, you can apply for multiple positions, but you should only apply for positions you're genuinely interested in and qualified for. Make sure to put effort into each application.

Where can I find server rules and guidelines?

Server rules and guidelines are posted in our Discord server. Make sure to read them carefully before joining the server to ensure you understand our community standards.

How do I contact staff if I have questions?

You can contact staff through our Discord ticket system. Navigate to the contact page on our website, which will redirect you to the appropriate Discord channel for support.

What makes Georgia State Roleplay different from other servers?

We focus on quality over quantity, maintain organized systems, and create a welcoming environment for all roleplayers. Our semi-whitelisted system ensures quality while remaining accessible, and our staff team is dedicated to supporting the community.
